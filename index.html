Project Brief

A mobile marketplace that allows users to buy, sell and barter items with consumers, local merchants and businesses, All while processing transactions directly on it's platform.

The Goal

Allow users to buy, sell, trade items or book services and receive these items within minutes through a delivery means integrated into the app.

Design Thinking

The development of the E-commerce app was shaped by UX principles. It helped me to keep focus on users and their needs.

What can users do in the app?

  • The users can buy, sell, switch items or book services.
  • Users can have their items shipped within minutes to their various destinations through an in-built delivery method.
  • In-app messaging for users to communicate safely with one another.
  • Users can process payment directly through the app.
